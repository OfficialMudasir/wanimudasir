OrionSTEM Schools

OrionSTEM Schools is a content-rich, high-traffic education website that supports discovery, admissions, and community engagement for a K–12 STEM school.

The work focuses on delivering a clean information architecture, fast page experiences, and a consistent UX across pages such as admissions, programs, outcomes, blogs/news, and contact flows—so families can quickly find the right information and take action.
